McIlroy headlines as Australian Open returns to Royal Melbourne

December 3, 2025

The 108th edition of the Australian Open begins this week at Royal Melbourne, marking the first time since 1991 that the tournament returns to this historic venue. Five time major champion Rory McIlroy leads the field, raising expectations among fans for a thrilling competition. Other top contenders include Adam Scott, Cam Smith, and Min Woo Lee, all aiming to reclaim the Stonehaven Cup for Australia. The shift back to traditional format has injected renewed excitement into one of golf's oldest tournaments.

Underwater chess: Niemann wins Diving Chess exhibition in Cape Town

December 4, 2025

Elite chess player Hans Niemann took home victory in a unique “Diving Chess” exhibition in Cape Town, where players make moves while underwater. The event part of the Freestyle Chess Grand Slam blends strategic depth with physical endurance, offering a fresh twist on traditional chess. Niemann's win marks a promising start to the tournament, drawing significant global attention to this unconventional sport format. The main Freestyle Chess tournament is scheduled to begin soon with a lineup of top world players.

Colby Covington vs. Luke Rockhold to headline RAF 5 event in Florida

December 4, 2025

Former UFC fighters Colby Covington and Luke Rockhold are set to clash at RAF 5 on January 10, 2026, in Sunrise, Florida. The fight marks Covington's return after a long hiatus following a doctor stoppage knockout in late 2024. Rockhold, meanwhile, aims to bounce back from recent losses in combat sports in an effort to re-establish his reputation. The bout has generated high anticipation among combat-sports fans, and will be streamed live.
